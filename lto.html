LTO Processing

In addition to our comprehensive insurance services, we also offer Land Transportation Office (LTO) processing to make managing your vehicle documentation seamless and hassle-free. Our LTO processing service handles all the necessary paperwork and procedures, ensuring that your vehicle is properly registered and compliant with all legal requirements. By choosing us, you benefit from a one-stop solution for both insurance and vehicle documentation, saving you time and effort while ensuring peace of mind on the road.

Requirements for our LTO Renewal Processing for Vehicle

Note: Our agency will assist anytime to completing the requirements:

  • Official Receipt OR given by the LTO (Photocopy or Original Documents)
  • Certificate of Registration CR given by the LTO (Photocopy or Original Documents)
  • Emission Test Result (Original Results)
  • Insurance COC (Given in our agency, Original Documents)
  • Stencil Form (Given by the emission test center or in our agency, Photocopy or Original Form)

    • Note: Additional requirements for tricyle For-Hire:

  • LTO copy of MTOP Form (Given by the city hall, Photocopy or Origainal copy)
  • Official Receipt OR of MTOP (Given by the city hall, Orignal copy)

Requirements for our LTO Processing for Transfer of Ownership

Note: Our Agency will assist anytime to completing the requirements:

  • Official Receipt OR given by the LTO (Photocopy or Original Documents)
  • Certificate of Registration CR given by the LTO (Photocopy or Original Documents)
  • Original copy of Deed of Sale with notary (We have also DOS incase of without DOS document)
  • Photocopy of 2 valid of the Vendor with 3 signature
  • Photocopy of 2 valid of the Vendee with 3 signature
